Privacy Policy of Start to Stable Ventures (the Company)

Data Collected: The Company collects information provided to us by interaction with potential Clients, Clients, potential Partners, Partners and / or through our digital communication channels e.g. information is collected when an account is created, emailer lists are populated, sales leads engaged, an order is placed, or invoice generated. The types of information collected might include client trading names, addresses and contact details, and billing information for invoicing purposes.

Use of the Data: The Company only uses personal client information for account management, sales lead opportunities, sales negotiations and sales management.

Standards applied: The Company employs industry-standard best practices to protect against unauthorised access of/to stored personal information. The Company does not share personal information provided to us without consent, unless:

  • - Doing so is appropriate to carry out in The Company’s own interest;
  • - The Company believes it’s needed to enforce legal agreements or that it is legally required;
  • - The Company believes it’s needed to detect, prevent or address fraud, security or technical issues.

Sharing of Data: The Company does not share personal information with third parties. Aggregated, anonymised data is periodically transmitted to external services to help to improve the service to Clients.

Opt-Out, Communication Preferences: If you are included on any email or other communication and wish to no longer receive marketing or other related communications, please contact The Company on: info@start2stable.com.

Security: The Company takes reasonable steps to protect personally identifiable information from loss, misuse, and unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to the Privacy Policy: The Company may amend this Privacy policy from time to time. If major changes are made in the way information is collected, the Company will notify affected parties by means of email.
